FAQ

What is Arogya Samriddhi card Membership and why must I get it?

We believe good quality and affordable healthcare for a healthy and dignified living is everyones basic right thus, with Arogya Samriddhi card, our aim is to cater to even those individuals for whom good quality healthcare has become un-affordable post-covid, due to extremely high inflation caused by disrupted supply chains from global wars, increased global warming events, rising unemployment and shrinking disposable incomes especially in lower and middle income groups in India while immunity, health-related activity levels and Family level savings are at all-time low causing extreme uncertainties and anxiety. With Arogya Samriddhi card membership, youre assured of getting Great quality of Healthcare from Leading institutions and experts near you at all times on a discount ranging anywhere from 10% to 40%. Meaning youll be visiting a hospital/doctor/diagnostic chain of your choice to get your regular procedure done but at an un-precedented discount using our card membership applicable for whole family making it an irresistible and a no-brainer deal.

Who can be included in an Arogya Samriddhi card membership?
Every family member in blood relationship can be added to the card membership irrespective of their age and location with a maximum of 8 members. Each card membership will have a primary member and rest will be associate family members who will jointly be availing the services.
What is the cost and the validity period of this membership?

There are two membership cards, one for a family of up-to 4 members costing Rs. 400 + GST and another for a family of 5 to 8 members costing Rs. 600 + GST both having same services and features being valid for 1 year.

How does our Arogya Samriddhi card membership service works?
Arogya Samriddhi card Members will be able to see the list of all empanelled doctors, hospitals, diagnostic chains, pharmacies and more in the app and on Company website. They can filter based on region, city, speciality type, distance from the users location and discounts offered etc. and can make appointments for visit as and when required. A user just needs to produce their physical membership card or digital membership card (inside the App) or Aadhar card for verification and documentation of visit and once the procedure is done, make payment for the service availed using our app or using regular payment methods. While making payment, user will be required to pay discounted price instead of the full price at our healthcare partners. This can be done as many times as needed during the membership period by any or all of the family members.
What kind of services and features are included in the membership?
  • OPD (all departments) at partner Healthcare providers
  • IPD (All departments) at partner Healthcare providers
  • All kinds of Screening tests at partner diagnostic chains
  • All Pathology tests at partner diagnostic chains
  • Ambulance Services
  • Blood Banks
  • All Partner Pharmacy chains
  • Physio & other Wellness services
  • Dieticians & Nutritionists
  • Dental services etc.
What kind of services and features are included in the membership?
Everyone can avail Arogya Samriddhi card membership with no exclusions. Even if youre having pre-existing ailments or are a smoker or have any other lifestyle disease, youre eligible for all benefits right from the date of membership for next 1 year.
How to avail the benefits of the Arogya Samriddhi Card membership?
Our members can book an appointment with desired partner hospital chains (as per their protocols) directly from Arogya Samriddhi App or by calling our customer service team member. In cases where our Healthcare partners allow direct walk-ins like in case of diagnostic chains, OPDs, Pharmacies etc. our members can simply visit, show card (either in physical form or in digital form from App) or alternatively share their Aadhar Card for verification and avail the services desired.
Am I eligible for the membership benefits if I already have health insurance or am covered in Ayushman Bharat scheme?
Yes, you can avail all the benefits of Arogya Samriddhi card Membership irrespective of all other schemes and coverages you may be part of, as most of the insurers and schemes dont provide coverage for OPD, medicine purchases, diagnostic tests, ambulances, physio and other wellness services and even in cases of IPD, there are deductions where our membership can still help you get discounts on your out of pocket expenses which you can easily calculate using our savings calculator on website.
How are the discounts determined and calculated for a member?
Arogya Samriddhi card members will be able to see applicable discount for any healthcare service provider as a whole or based on the procedure (as applicable) directly from the app. The discounts range anywhere from 10% to 40% and the same can be availed while making payments, which can be made either from App or using any regular payment method like card, UPI, wallets etc.
How big is our partner healthcare service provider network currently and how big itll get eventually?
A Journey of a thousand miles starts with a single step so to begin with, just like any other business were starting humbly with a few districts and limited partnerships in the region however we aim to spread at a break-neck speed to multiple states and eventually Pan-India within a period of 1 year or so, with help of a large Franchise network & our own workforce and strong digital infrastructure ensuring that we cover most geographic regions and service types (as mentioned in above question) so our members can avail best value for their membership cost and make huge savings while availing Top class Healthcare services
Will there be any discount on renewal if I dont use the card during the year?
Yes, such members can avail a direct discount of 20% on renewal fee for next membership year when renewed without any service break (while membership is still in effect).
How can I refer someone and what are the benefits?
Every Arogya Samriddhi card member will get a unique membership number and a unique referral code which can be used to generate referral link for ones friends, relatives and acquaintances and once a new member joins the card membership (after downloading the app and paying the fees), referring member will be able to get the referral bonus of Rs. 100 credited to their wallet account in the App.
Is there any limit to the referral bonus earnings & how can I use said referral bonus amount?
No, there is no limit to how many referrals can one make and how much referral bonus amount a card member can earn. Also, such bonus amount will be credit to users wallet linked to Arogya Samriddhi App and this amount can be used to pay for renewal fees or avail any service at partner healthcare service providers.
