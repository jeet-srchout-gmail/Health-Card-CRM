      • a. Consultations: Access to general practitioners, specialists, and consultations for non-emergency conditions.

      • i. Blood Tests: Routine blood work, including cholesterol levels, blood sugar, etc.

      • iii. Screening Tests: Regular health screening tests to detect potential risks like cancer, heart disease, and diabetes.
    • 4. Pharmacy (OTC & Prescription Medicines): Cardholders can avail of discounts on OTC and prescription medicines at partnered pharmacies.
      • i. Prescription Medicines: Discounts on essential medicines prescribed by healthcare professionals.

      • iii. Home Delivery: Many pharmacies in the network offer home delivery for prescriptions and essential medications.

      • i. Diagnostic Services: Access to medical imaging centres for procedures such as MRI, CT scan, X-rays, and more.

      • iii. Home-Based Diagnostics: Certain tests may also be available for home collection based on the user’s needs and health requirements.
    • 6. All Types of Screening Tests: Arogya Samriddhi offers members various screening tests for early detection.
      • i. Cancer Screenings: Early detection tests for common cancers such as breast, cervical, prostate, and others.
      • ii. Heart Health Screenings: Tests like cholesterol levels, ECG, and stress tests.
      • iii. Diabetes Screening: Blood sugar tests and diabetes risk assessments.
    • 7. Ambulance Services
      • In case of emergencies, the Arogya Samriddhi Card provides access to ambulance services. This service ensures that in critical medical situations, members can be transported to the nearest hospital or healthcare facility without delay.
      • i. Emergency Ambulance: Coverage for emergency transport services, including life-saving equipment like oxygen and basic first-aid during transportation.
      • ii. Non-Emergency Ambulance: For patients requiring transport to medical facilities for scheduled treatments or check-ups.
      • iii. 24/7 Availability: Ambulance services are available around the clock to ensure members have access to timely medical assistance.
    • 8. Dental Care

      • i. Routine Check-ups: Regular dental check-ups to monitor oral health and prevent problems.
      • ii. Dental Treatments: Coverage for common dental procedures such as fillings, tooth extractions, and cleanings.
      • iii. Orthodontics & Cosmetic Dentistry: Discounts on services like braces and teeth whitening for those looking to improve their smile.
    • 9. Dieticians and Nutritionists
      • The card also provides members with access to professional dieticians and nutritionists who can help guide them toward healthier eating habits, manage chronic conditions, and work towards a balanced lifestyle.
      • i. Nutrition Counselling: Personalized advice to manage weight, blood sugar levels, and other health conditions.
      • ii. Meal Plans: Customized meal planning for improved health and nutrition.
      • iii. Chronic Disease Management: Support for managing conditions like hypertension, diabetes, and obesity through diet and lifestyle adjustments.
    • 10. Physiotherapy and Wellness Services
      • The Arogya Samriddhi Card includes access to physiotherapy and wellness services designed to aid in recovery, pain management, and general well-being. These services are aimed at individuals who are recovering from injuries or those looking to improve their overall physical health.
      • i. Physiotherapy Sessions: Personalized sessions for rehabilitation and injury recovery.
      • ii. Wellness Programs: Yoga, meditation, and other therapies designed to improve mental and physical health.
      • iii. Post-Surgery Recovery: Services designed to aid in recovery following surgeries or injuries.

      • i. 24/7 Customer Support: Round-the-clock assistance to address any issues or questions.
